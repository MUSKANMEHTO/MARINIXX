About MERENIX

Advancing marine research through integrated data and artificial intelligence

Revolutionizing Marine Data Analysis

MERENIX is a cutting-edge platform designed to unify disparate marine data sources into a single, accessible interface. By leveraging advanced AI algorithms, we transform complex oceanographic, fisheries, and biodiversity data into actionable insights.

Our mission is to democratize access to marine data and accelerate scientific discovery by breaking down data silos and providing powerful analytical tools.

Use Cases

Real-world applications of MERENIX across marine sectors

Sustainable Fisheries

Optimize fishing efforts, predict stock fluctuations, and implement ecosystem-based fisheries management using integrated data on ocean conditions, fish populations, and harvesting patterns.

Biodiversity Conservation

Identify biodiversity hotspots, track changes in species distribution, and evaluate conservation effectiveness by combining traditional survey data with cutting-edge eDNA analysis.

Blue Economy

Support sustainable marine economic development by providing data-driven insights for aquaculture site selection, renewable energy planning, and tourism management.
